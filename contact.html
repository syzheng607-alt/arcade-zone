Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Just click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in your details, and you'll be ready to start gaming in minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin and Ethereum. All payments are processed securely.

Can I get a refund for a game?

Yes! We offer a 14-day refund policy for games that have been played for less than 2 hours. Contact our support team to initiate a refund.

Are the games compatible with my device?

Most of our games are web-based and work on any modern browser. Some games may have specific system requirements listed on their individual pages.

How often do you add new games?

We add new games every week! Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on the latest releases.
